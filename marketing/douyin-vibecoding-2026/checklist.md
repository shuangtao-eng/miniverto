# Publishing Checklist

## Before Posting

- [ ] Title starts with `VibeCoding大赏`.
- [ ] Caption includes `#vibecoding大赏`.
- [ ] Caption includes `#ai新星计划`.
- [ ] Caption includes `@抖音科技`.
- [ ] Image-text post has at least 100 Chinese characters.
- [ ] Image-text post includes at least 3 images.
- [ ] Video post is at least 1 minute long.
- [ ] Play the MP4 once on phone or desktop to confirm voice and BGM balance.
- [ ] GitHub URL is included in the caption or first comment.
- [ ] The post says Miniverto is still alpha, so expectations are honest.
